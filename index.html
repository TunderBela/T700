T-700

Logistics & Sorting Unit

USER MANUAL

Welcome, Operator. Control the T-700 unit to organize the mail depot.

  • Controls: Use Arrow keys / WASD on PC, or the touch buttons on mobile.
  • Envelopes: Step over them to collect. You can carry multiple at once. Deliver them to Mailboxes.
  • Boxes: Push them one by one into the Loading Lorry.
  • Switches & Doors: Step on switches to toggle door security states.
